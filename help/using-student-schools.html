The Schools table contains the schools which the student has requested to be sent a recommendation.

Schools Field Definitions

The school name and required by date fields are required.. The rest are optional; and.

  • School The university title (i.e., Georgia Tech).
  • School Program/Position The degree or position desired. Carefully describe the degree/program. (i.e., Masters in Business Administration, Research Assistant)
  • School Contact Email Email address of the school contact.
  • School Contact Postal The address of the USPS address of the school contact. (i.i., Office of Admissions //One Brookings Drive //PO Box 777 //St. Joseph, MO 55121-0403 //Re: Carl Schwartz)
  • Status The status of the school recommendation.
    • Initial - Just entered by the student. No activity yet
    • Open - Some sork has been done. Probably a recommendlate has been created.
    • Sent/Notify - The recommendation has been sent to the school
    • Closed - All activity completed.
  • Date Due Latest date required by the school. When a student indicaate the date, it must be at least 30 days after the request was mase.
  • Professor Comments Any comments that the professor notes pertaining to the school. It will show on the initial recommendlate.
  • Timestamp Not modifiable. Timestamp of the last update to the record.
