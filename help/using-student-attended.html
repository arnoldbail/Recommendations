The Student Courses Enrolled table contains the courses that the student indicated he/she had enrolled that were conducted by the professor. This includes the course number and semester/year. The professor comments are aded at the discretion of the professor; and, if present, are on the initial Recommendlate.

Student Courses Enrolled Field Definitions

The Course Number, semester, year are required fields. The rest are optional; and, if present are displayed on the initial recommendlate.

  • Course Number The course number of the enrolled course.
  • Semester adress The semester the course was taken.
  • Year The year the course was taken(i.e., 2012)
  • Professor Comments Comments entered by the professor peraining to the student during the course. These will be added to the initial Recommendlate.
  • Timestamp Not modifiable. Timestamp of the last update to the record.
